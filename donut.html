Pink Paradise

A strawberry-flavored donut with a smooth, glossy pink glaze, topped with colorful candy sprinkles.

All-purpose flour (bleached wheat flour, malted barley flour, niacin, iron, thiamine mononitrate, riboflavin, folic acid), butter (pasteurized cream, salt), sugar, milk, eggs, yeast, vegetable oil (palm and/or canola), strawberry glaze (sugar, corn syrup, water, natural flavors, citric acid, artificial color [red 40]), colored sprinkles (sugar, corn starch, palm oil, natural and artificial colors, soy lecithin), salt, natural vanilla extract, baking powder, water.

Contains: Milk, wheat, egg, soy

Our donuts are made onsite and may come into contact with different allergens during production. Please be advised that any of our products may contain allergens including peanuts, tree nuts, milk, eggs, wheat, soy, and sesame.
